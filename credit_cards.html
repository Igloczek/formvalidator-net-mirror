Our credit card validation service is intended to be used on ecommerce web sites that do not have a credit card payment gateway. Fully functional credit card gateways are expensive to implement. We offer a alternative solution for you to take payments from your web site (forms) without having to implement a payment gateway.

Like the rest of our services, implementation of our credit card payment solution requires adding only one (1) line of code to your web form. Your customers never leave your web site

Our suite of services provide the unique and powerful feature of blocking your form submission when someone provides bad or undesirable information. This has always been a challenge for web site designers and business process owners. All of our solutions provide real-time, on-line validation from authoritative sources.

Pricing for our payment solution is competitive and depends upon your transaction volume. Please contact us directly for pricing.

Because our payment solution is a web service it is possible to implement on many different domains or sites and one time!. For example: If you had a large telemarketing campaign out-sourced to several different overflow call centers you could instruct each if these call centers to copy your Validator payment form. This would facilitate your acceptance of credit card payments simultaneously from all call centers. And you would incur implementation cost because The Form Validator does everything.
